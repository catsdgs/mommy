About

Hi. My name is Jennifer, and I am a freelance proofreader. Along with a passion for the written word, I am also a foodie, a basketball coach, and a music lover.


I have always loved to read and write, and it was this passion that eventually led me to pursue a degree in English. After college, I managed a local bookstore for six years. I enjoyed it a lot, because I got the opportunity to read and review countless books from a wide range of genres. Then, after receiving a Master's degree in Educational Counseling, I worked as a high school counselor for three years, where I helped students edit and proofread college application essays.


I possess...

  • a mastery of the English language

  • a stong eye for detail

  • a firm grasp of writing style and tone

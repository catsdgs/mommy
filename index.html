About ProofReadAble

I am a freelance proofreader with strong background in women's literature and student essays. I have always been passionate about reading & writing. Because of that passion, I chose to study Language Arts in college and earn a Bachelor's degree in English. Along with a mastery of the English language, I also have a strong eye for detail and the ability to spot words used in the wrong context. I also possess a firm grasp of writing style and tone.
